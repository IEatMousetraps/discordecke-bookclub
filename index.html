Who We Are

Server 96 is a Columbus Crew supporters group that started as an online community on the Discordecke Discord server. Founded by three moderators, we grew from digital friendships into real-life matchday camaraderie, from tailgates to the Nordecke and beyond.

Our Origins

The Discordecke Discord brought Crew fans together online. The community recognized the potential and founded Server 96 to give it a real-world identity, a place where digital connections became genuine friendships.

The Nordecke

Many of our members group together in the Nordecke, the heart of Crew supporter culture. Others sit in different sections, but we always make noise together. On matchday, we are one.

Tailgates & Matchdays

We organize tailgates, watch parties, and meetups for Columbus Crew home and away games. Every match is an opportunity to grow the community and celebrate Black & Gold soccer.

Community First

Server 96 exists to promote the Discordecke and connect like-minded Crew supporters. Whether you're local or watching from afar, there's a place for you in our group.
